We invest in the transformation of used-car marketplaces

We support local entrepreneurs to build succesful companies using a scalable, innovative business model and platform.

We’re well placed in massive, demographically favourable markets as they transition to consumption led growth.

We design and develop digital marketplaces, enabling our ventures to disrupt their used-automotive markets.

How it works

Seller gets an online quote.

Our advanced digital quote engine gives users an instant price for their vehicle and allows them to quickly book an inspection at one of our locations.

We inspect and purchase the car.

Our inspectors perform a 126-point inspection of the vehicle, make a final offer and complete the sale. Then, using panoramic imaging technology, we create photographs ready for sale.

Going once, going twice…

Our dealer network uses our secure digital bidding platform to compete in an auction for the vehicle. All our vehicles are tested with certified paperwork, leaving dealers to concentate on geting the best price for their chosen models.

Explore our markets

We’ve over 600 people across five existing operational markets, with additional staff in Sweden, the US and our Berlin HQ.
